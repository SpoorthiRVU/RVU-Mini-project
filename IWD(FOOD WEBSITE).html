Vegan Falafel Recipe

Enjoy this delicious and easy-to-make vegan falafel at home!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 1/2 large onion, roughly chopped (about 1 cup)
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 4 tablespoons flour (use chickpea flour or all-purpose flour)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak the chickpeas in water overnight.
  2. Drain and rinse the chickpeas.
  3. Combine chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, coriander, cumin, baking powder, flour, salt, and pepper in a food processor.
  4. Blend until the mixture has a coarse texture and holds together.
  5. Form small patties from the mixture.
  6. Heat oil in a skillet and fry the falafel until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Remove falafel from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!

Hara Bhara Kebab Recipe

Veg Fatayer Jebnah Recipe

Enjoy the savory goodness of Veg Fatayer Jebnah!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, tomatoes, onions, etc.), finely chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, olive oil, salt, and the yeast mixture. Knead until you get a smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together chopped vegetables, feta cheese, parsley, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  6. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a circle and place a spoonful of the vegetable-cheese mixture in the center.
  7. Fold the edges of the dough to form a triangular shape, pinching the edges to seal.
  8. Place the filled triangles on a baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven, let them cool slightly, and serve your Veg Fatayer Jebnah!

Zaatar Manakeesh Recipe

Enjoy the flavorful Veg Zaatar Manakeesh, a perfect blend of herbs and spices!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons zaatar spice mix
  • 1/4 cup olive oil (for the zaatar topping)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, olive oil, salt, and the yeast mixture. Knead until you get a smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  5. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into small balls.
  6. Roll each ball into a flat circle and place it on a baking sheet.
  7. Mix zaatar spice with olive oil and spread it evenly on top of each dough circle.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and enjoy your Veg Zaatar Manakeesh!

Arabic Lentil Soup Recipe

Enjoy the comforting warmth of Arabic Lentil Soup!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic; sauté until softened.
  2. Add carrots and celery to the pot; cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Rinse lentils and add them to the pot. Stir in cumin, coriander, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer until lentils are tender (about 20-25 minutes).
  5. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, blend in batches in a regular blender and return to the pot.
  6. Squeeze in the lemon juice and adjust salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your Arabic Lentil Soup!

Paneer Tikka Recipe

Enjoy the delicious flavors of homemade Paneer Tikka!

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon chaat masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced into rings
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, chaat masala, lemon juice, salt, and vegetable oil to make the marinade.
  2. Add paneer cubes to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread marinated paneer cubes, onion rings, and bell pepper slices onto skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally until the paneer is golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and enjoy your homemade Paneer Tikka!

Vegetarian Moussaka Recipe

Indulge in the rich flavors of Vegetarian Moussaka!

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • For the Béchamel Sauce:
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and nutmeg to taste
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brush eggplant and potato slices with olive oil and roast them in the oven until golden brown.
  3. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic until softened. Add zucchini and bell peppers, cooking until vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. For the Béchamel sauce, melt butter in a saucepan, whisk in flour until smooth, then gradually add milk. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and nutmeg. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  6. Layer half of the roasted eggplant and potato slices in a baking dish, followed by the vegetable mixture. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of vegetables.
  7. Pour the Béchamel sauce over the layers. If desired, sprinkle shredded mozzarella on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and enjoy your Vegetarian Moussaka!

Veg Molokhia Recipe

Enjoy the rich and flavorful Veg Molokhia!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped Jew's mallow leaves (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Cooked rice or bread for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic; sauté until softened.
  2. Add carrots, potatoes, and zucchini to the pot; cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, ground coriander, ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Add Jew's mallow leaves to the pot and stir until they wilt.
  5. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve the Veg Molokhia over cooked rice or with bread.
  7. Enjoy your Veg Molokhia!

Veg Koshari Recipe

Enjoy the hearty and flavorful Veg Koshari!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice, washed
  • 1 cup brown lentils, washed
  • 1 cup short pasta (macaroni or penne)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Topping:
  • Fried onions
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • Garlic-infused vinegar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook basmati rice and brown lentils separately according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, sauté chopped onions and minced garlic in vegetable oil until softened.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper to the pan. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Layer the cooked rice, lentils, and pasta in a serving dish. Pour the tomato sauce over the layers.
  6. Top with fried onions and chopped fresh parsley.
  7. Optionally, drizzle garlic-infused vinegar on top for extra flavor.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your Veg Koshari!

Om Ali Recipe

Indulge in the sweet and comforting flavors of Om Ali!

Ingredients:

  • 4 sheets of puff pastry, baked and broken into pieces
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mixed nuts (almonds, pistachios, and hazelnuts), chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream or vanilla ice cream for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Bake the puff pastry sheets according to the package instructions. Once baked, break them into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it's warm. Add sugar and vanilla extract, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
  4. In a baking dish, layer half of the broken puff pastry pieces.
  5. Sprinkle half of the chopped nuts and shredded coconut over the pastry layer.
  6. Pour half of the warm milk mixture over the layers, ensuring the puff pastry absorbs the liquid.
  7. Repeat the layers with the remaining puff pastry, nuts, coconut, and milk mixture.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the dessert is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and serve Om Ali warm. Optionally, top with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.
  10. Enjoy your sweet treat!

Turkish Coffee Cake Recipe

Indulge in the exotic flavors of Turkish Coffee with this delightful cake!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup strong brewed Turkish coffee, cooled
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • For the Coffee Glaze:
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons strong brewed Turkish coffee, cooled
  • Chopped pistachios for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the Greek yogurt and brewed Turkish coffee.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cardamom, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. While the cake is cooling, prepare the coffee glaze by whisking together powdered sugar and brewed Turkish coffee until smooth.
  9. Once the cake has cooled, drizzle the coffee glaze over the top and garnish with chopped pistachios if desired.
  10. Slice and enjoy your delicious Turkish Coffee Cake!

Chicken Shawarma Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Flatbreads or pita for serving
  • Toppings: Chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, lettuce, tahini sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the sliced chicken, sliced onion, minced garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the yogurt, olive oil, and lemon juice to the chicken mixture. Mix well, ensuring the chicken is coated with the spices and marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  3. Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken until fully cooked and slightly crispy on the edges.
  4. Warm the flatbreads or pita and assemble the shawarma by placing the cooked chicken on the bread. Add your favorite toppings and drizzle with tahini sauce.

Chicken Mansaf Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup ghee or clarified butter
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 5 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the chicken pieces, yogurt, ghee, chopped onion, water, salt, pepper, cinnamon, cardamom, and allspice. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until the chicken is tender (about 1.5 to 2 hours).
  2. In a separate pot, cook the rice according to package instructions.
  3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pot and set aside. Keep the cooking liquid.
  4. In a large serving dish, arrange the cooked rice. Place the chicken pieces on top.
  5. Pour some of the cooking liquid over the rice and chicken. Reserve the rest of the liquid to serve on the side.
  6. Toast the pine nuts in a pan until golden brown and sprinkle them over the chicken and rice.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot. Serve the remaining cooking liquid on the side for drizzling as desired.

Samak Harra Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg whole fish (such as sea bass or snapper), cleaned and scaled
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rinse the fish and pat it dry. Make three diagonal cuts on each side.
  3. In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, coriander, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  4. Rub the fish with the spice mixture, making sure to get into the cuts.
  5. Place the fish in a baking dish. Pour any remaining spice mixture over the top.
  6. In a separate bowl, combine chopped tomatoes, cilantro, parsley, and lemon juice. Spoon the mixture over the fish.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the fish is cooked through.

Sayadieh Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg white fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed and soaked
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups fish or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish
  • Fried almonds for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown and caramelized. Remove half of the onions and set aside for garnish.
  2. Add rice to the pot with the remaining onions, and sauté for a few minutes until the rice is coated with oil.
  3. Add fish fillets to the pot, placing them on top of the rice.
  4. In a bowl, mix fish or vegetable broth with cinnamon, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the fish and rice.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the fish is flaky (about 15-20 minutes).
  6. Garnish with the reserved caramelized onions, chopped parsley, and fried almonds if desired.

Al Faham Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cleaned and cut into pieces

  • - 1/4 cup yogurt
  • - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • - 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 tablespoon ground coriander

  • - 1 teaspoon paprika

Shakshuka Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish
  • Feta cheese for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté until softened.
  2. Add diced bell pepper and minced garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and add cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Create small wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them.
  5. Cover the skillet and let the eggs poach in the sauce for about 5-7 minutes or until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and feta cheese. Serve hot with crusty bread.

Foul Medames Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked fava beans
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish
  • Optional toppings: Chopped green onions, radishes, tahini

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add cooked fava beans and diced tomatoes to the saucepan. Cook for a few minutes until heated through.
  3. In a separate pan, fry the eggs to your liking.
  4. Mash the fava bean mixture slightly with a fork, leaving some beans whole.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste.
  6. Serve the fava bean mixture in bowls, topped with fried eggs.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley and add optional toppings if desired. Serve with pita bread.
